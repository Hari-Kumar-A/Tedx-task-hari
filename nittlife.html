The Life at NITT

-A Hari Kumar

The life at NITT is really amazing. The top class and creative and passionate students study together at NITT.
The beautiful Orion building is key beauty of our college.We have land of 800+acres
The Mig located behind Orion is one of the beautiful and peaceful space to spend with friends.
The Octagon quote,"Dare to Dream" motivates me a lot

  • Orion is the lecutre hall for B Tech students. It is well planned and constructed by
    Architecture proffesionals and NITT passouts.

  • We have a very good sports complex which has tennis court, badminton court, kabaddi court,
    students running here and there, meant for physical fitness.
    Student must make use of these faciltiies to fullest.

  • The OCTAGON - Computer Center is the sterling hallmark of this campus was opened
    with the theme of "Dare to Dream". The OCTAGON & TwinNet (Annex1) and
    Third i (Annex2) buildings accommodates the central computing facilities,
    to solve tough questions of Irodov, dirty calculations of Algebra what not!
